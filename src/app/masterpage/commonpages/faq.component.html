FAQ Do I still need to register on this portal if I have only few B2B tickets? All B2B invoices will be available on the portal What type of details/documents are required to register on the portal? A copy of the business PAN and GSTIN Can one corporate have multiple registration on this portal? No. One registration per PAN will be permitted. You may create multiple users per corporate and assign access to all or selected states Can a corporate/B2B customer allow his travel agent to download his B2B GST invoices? No What if I could not find my B2B GST invoice? We have a raise request section on the portal. You may submit the details of your purchase, and we will address the same If I find incorrect details on the GST invoice? We have a raise request section on the portal. You may submit the discrepancy details, and we will address the same How can I raise a query? Login to the portal and Click on the Raise request link on the left panel. Submit your query as for the provided form Can I download my invoice without a ticket number? Yes. You can download the invoices by just providing a date period. If you require the invoice for a particular ticket number, you can fill up the same.
